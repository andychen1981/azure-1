- Dedicated to fulfilling unmet needs of dialysis community




Committed to patient wellness


Started out the ideas in 2015 when came upon by physician friends who had often came up new ways to treat the patients so that they could be taken care of more humanely and comfortably. But without the necessary devices or business experiences, physicians just gave up and threw the many good ideas that never came to see the light.

In 2017, we incepted the company to carry on several marketing survey proved there were medical unmet needs there, with the little private funding we had, we then developed the prototype to prove our concept was effective.

We are a big pool of top talents from medical doctors, research experts, fast turn-around prototype builders, AI data scientists and software developers, etc.

In order to safely and widely meet the patient needs, we set our product goal to always follow the local regulatory compliances.

Our mission is to avoid the thrombosis to happen, instead early detection of stenosis, a less serious condition, and have the simpler and extremely less painful treatment performed
